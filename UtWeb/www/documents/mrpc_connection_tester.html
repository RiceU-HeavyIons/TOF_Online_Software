MRPC Connection Tester
Overview
This simple device is desinged to test short/continuity of all combinations of MRPC module connector pin pairs. 16bit microcontroller (PIC16F913) manages four 16-channel multiplexiers (MAX306), selects pin pair, measures registance across two pins, and finally indicates error by LEDs.
